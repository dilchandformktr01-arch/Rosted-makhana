Dandkhora krishi Pragti Producer Company

State

Bihar

Total Members

1558

Date of Incorporation

05 JANUARY 2023

Key Business Activity

Production & Marketing

Share Capital

INR 0.3Cr

Women Members

100%

Business Model

The FPC was promoted to help farmers avoid distress sale and improve market access opportunities. The main crops include paddy, green gram and mahua.

Interventions on Production Side

  • Training & capacity building on agronomic practices
  • Access to subsidized seeds and fertilizers
  • Seed production support from Odisha State Seeds Corporation
  • Reduced cost of production by 5–7%

Market Linkages

  • Foundation seeds sold back to Odisha State Seeds Corporation
  • Aggregation of pulses and mahua for better prices
  • Warehouse storage during low market price periods

Benefits of Aggregation

  • Turnover of INR 4.5 crore in 2024-25
  • Net profit of INR 5.6 lakh
  • Improved farmer income and reduced distress sales
  • Access to finance through NABARD and State bank of India

SWOT Analysis

Strength

Farmer responsiveness to training and capacity building

Weakness

Lack of infrastructure and limited institutional linkage

Opportunity

Seed processing and alternate market channels

Threat

Supply fluctuations and mandi dependency

Main Activity

Production, Processing & Marketing

Business Model

BDVPCL focuses on improving livelihoods of small and marginal farmers through production, processing and value addition in pulses and vegetables.

Production Side Interventions

  • Climate-friendly and organic farming methods
  • 100% seed replacement with improved local varieties
  • Promotion of vermicompost and bio-fertilizers
  • Training with KVK and agricultural institutions

Market Linkages

  • Collection centers established by the FPC
  • Aggregated produce sold at better market rates
  • Future plans for branding and packaging

Impact & Benefits

  • Profit of INR 1.2 crore in 2019-20
  • 636 farmers increased income by INR 20,000 annually
  • Improved infrastructure and financial literacy
  • Employment generation for local youth

SWOT Analysis

Strength

Fertile soil and strong market demand

Weakness

Need for post-harvest infrastructure

Opportunity

Branding, processing and value addition

Threat

Perishable produce and supply fluctuations
